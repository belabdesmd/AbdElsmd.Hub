#### Chill
Be chill, relaxed and take a pause before Acting. That would move your brain from the Reaction mode into Action mode where you are more able of taking control and not leaning into Reflexes or Instinct.

#### Conscious
Be logical and rational. You should seek the most logical action instead of leaning into your Instinct (Pick what's right, not what you like).

#### Committed
Stick to your decision even it hurt, it is boring or it is hard.
